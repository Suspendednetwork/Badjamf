# What BYOA is 

It Stands for (Bring your own app) 

What Jamf scans to block a app its App behaviour Signature and Bundle ids 

Say you fork [Playcover](https://github.com/PlayCover/PlayCover) And directly download it. Jamf will recognise the app and block it, 

But if you change the Bundle ID, Signature and App behaviour Jamf will turn a blind eye to the app Until a administrator looks at the app and blocks it
